# Workflows

## Time Logging
1. Staff logs time for assigned project
2. Data stored as time log

---

## Report Submission
1. Staff submits daily report
2. System checks:
   - If within 48 hours → PENDING
   - If after 48 hours → LATE
3. Report becomes locked (non-editable)

---

## Approval
1. Project owner or supervisor reviews
2. Approve or reject report

---

## Payment Calculation
1. Use approved and late reports
2. Apply cost per hour
3. Store result as payment record

---

## Dashboard
1. Aggregate time logs
2. Show:
   - Monthly total hours
   - Hours per project
